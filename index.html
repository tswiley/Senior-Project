Where Community & Culture Meet

Red Leg Brewing Company is proud to be a part of the Colorado Springs community. We are committed to giving back to the community that has given us so much. We are proud to support the following organizations:

Beer

Step into our taproom to experience a diverse selection of made-in-house craft beers. From rich and robust stouts to crisp and refreshing ales, each pour encapsulates our dedication to quality and innovation. We also proudly feature local guest taps, ciders, kombuchas, and wines to cater to every palate.

Food Trucks

Savor the local flavor at our brewery and embark on a culinary adventure with our diverse lineup of food trucks. From mouthwatering savory dishes to irresistible sweets, our community gathering is a celebration of delicious moments. Join us for an unforgettable experience of craft brews, good company, and the best bites our local food trucks have to offer.

What's Happening?

Our Makers Markets feature a rotating selection of local artists, bakers, makers, and farmers. A fun combination of farm and art market! Head on over to the brewery for our outdoor market and support small local businesses all in one convenient stop!
